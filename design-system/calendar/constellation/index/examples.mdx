## Default

The calendar component provides a way to render dates for selection or presentation purposes.

<Example Component={CalendarDefault} packageName="@atlaskit/calendar" />

## Disabled

Calendar provides a `disabled` prop that accepts an array of arbitrary dates to disable.

<Example Component={CalendarDisabled} packageName="@atlaskit/calendar" />

## Disabled ranges

To disable all dates before or after a certain date, use `minDate` or `maxDate`. These props disable all dates before or after a specific day respectively.
Use a `minDate` and a `maxDate` together to create a range of days to choose from. Note that the min or max dates you choose will still be selectable (not disabled).

<Example Component={CalendarDisabledRange} packageName="@atlaskit/calendar" />

## Disabled filters

For even more control, use the `disabledDateFilter` prop, which accepts a callback that can be configured to return whether a given date is disabled or not.

<Example Component={CalendarDisabledFilter} packageName="@atlaskit/calendar" />

## Localization

The `locale` prop updates the calendar language and formatting for different locales.
You may also want to pair `locale` with the `weekStartDay` prop to change the day of the week the calendar starts with.

<Example Component={CalendarLocale} packageName="@atlaskit/calendar" />
